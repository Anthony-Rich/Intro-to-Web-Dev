Sigh No More

Mumford & Sons

October 2, 2009

Sigh No More was the bands debut album and included the hit single "Little Lion Man".The album has been a commericial success selling over 6 million copies to date and won a Brit Award for 'Album of the Year.'

Band Members

Marcus Mumfors - Lead Vocals, Drums
Ben Lovett - Keyboard, Piano
Winston Marshall - Rhythm guitar, Banjo, Backup Vocals
Ted Dwane - Bass guitar, Double Bass
